Show / Hide

To assign eCards, you may manually enter the student information OR you may upload a file containing the student information. To upload a file, click the "Upload Student List" button and choose the file you wish to upload.

NOTE: You must download a new template each time you upload a student list. The file must be in .xls or .csv format. Once the student information has been populated in the fields below, click the "Continue" button to proceed emailing eCards to students.
